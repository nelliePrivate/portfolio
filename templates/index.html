Wiwa

Whispering Wall, or Wiwa for short, is a chatbot built with python.

The NLTK library is utilized to parse user entered text and separate identified parts of speech into lists.
Once all the cleaning and parsing is done, one of the parsed parts of speech is selected at random.

A script that has sentences set up to fit that part of speech ((is also selected randomly to return to the user)).

If the code can't detect an English word, a script of silly phrases (Most from Monty Python) is used, and a random selection comes from there.

Wiwa can respond to her own name, greetings, my name, questions, simple answers, and I have recently added a list of words she will not tolerate being used in the chatbox. Go have a chat! It's just sillyness, I promise.
