About Chingu.io

Chingu is a learning and training platform where teams, or individuals, can participate in Voyages.

During a Voyage, members have 6 weeks to develop their project.

Throughout this process they will grow as a developer, but they also learn valuable lessons about community, communication, and building web applications as a team.

Wiwa

Whispering Wall, or Wiwa for short, is a chatbot built with python.
The NLTK library is utilized to parse user entered text,
and separate identified parts of speech into lists.
Once all the cleaning and parsing is done, one of the parsed parts of speech is selected at random.
A script that has sentences set up to fit that part of speech is also selected randomly to return to the user.
If the code can't detect an English word, a script of silly phrases (many from Monty Python) is used, and a random selection comes from there.
Wiwa can also respond to her own name, greetings, my name, questions, simple answers, and I have recently added a list of words she will not tolerate being used in the chatbox. Go have a chat! It's just silliness, I promise.
